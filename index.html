01 — About

Designer & developer with a soft spot for the details.

I'm a full-stack developer and UX/UI designer who turns complex problems into clean, intuitive products. I work end to end — close enough to Figma to sweat the kerning, deep enough in the stack to build the API behind it.

Over the last few years I've helped startups and teams craft products that feel considered, fast, and genuinely enjoyable to use. I care about accessibility, performance, and that hard-to-name quality that makes an interface feel right.
